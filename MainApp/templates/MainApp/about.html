About Us

I’m Xinwei, a visual artist based in Singapore. I make collaborative artworks with communities to explore ideas around memory, time and impermanence. I believe the best way to experience art is to make it, and that artworks are much more interesting when they’re not mounted on museum walls. I have lots of questions, and making art together with people is how I try to find answers.

I'm Chin Yang, a Software Engineer by day and artist/photographer/writer by night, midnight, and sometimes right before dawn. I love exploring the intersection between Art and Technology, and spend most of my weekends playing board games, creating works that fuse my various interests, and being a Tech peon for my friends and family. I am also the web developer for this project so please contact me if this sentence is not visible to you.
